Malay Traditional Famous Outdoor Games

A project that purpose to come out with various types of activities to promote the famous Malay traditional outdoor games.

Overview

In this era, the evolution of technology has increased rapidly without us realising it and is still evolving every day, leading to new things such as new games. Digital games or Online games have been famous for many years and people spend hours playing them with a wide variety of types and platforms. With this new kind of game, our traditional games have never gained any attention from our new generation because they rather spend their time playing video games. This will be a problem for our future soon because our younger generation will not know about our traditional games as they never get any exposure to them. Thus this project aims to prevent that from happening soon by giving exposure about our traditional games.

For our part, we will be presenting Malay Traditional Games which are from the Outdoor and Famous categories. We will be explaining the introduction of our type of traditional games, their background, and how to play them briefly. This is important to make sure they deeply understand and appreciate traditional games as they will find it is a fun game to play. The significance of our project is to help raise awareness among youngsters. This will prevent traditional games from vanishing from our history. As for the outcome, tradition still will be played by our future generation and will never be replaced with something new that will affect our unique culture.
